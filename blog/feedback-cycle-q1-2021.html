Fostering Development with our Feedback Cycle Q1/2021

In 2021 we as People & Organization Team do not only focus on the attraction of new talented Alascians, but also on the development of our existing team. We offer support in their personal and professional growth, because we deeply believe that people and their development are the core of the success of the company and that regular feedback is at the center of it.

Regular Feedback cycles and open and transparent communication are at the essential to the collaboration at Alasco. Our P&O Team has set up a semi-structured process with guidelines and tips on how to give and receive feedback to prepare all Team Leads for the task. More in-depth info can be found in our Alasco Inside Post on "How we give Feedback".

The new role of P&O: Development Sparring Partner

In the end of 2020 we started to rethink what development means to us as a company, but also for the People & Organization Team. At Alasco each employee has a yearly development budget, but we realized that it had not been used in many cases. The problem was - as we found out through an anonymous survey - that most employees didn’t know how to develop themselves and sometimes Team Leads and People Manager didn’t have the time or resources to support them as most of them have busy schedules.

Up until then the P&O Team did not play an active role in the development of Alascians, thus we decided that we wanted to shift the focus and support not only the employees, but also the team leads in their efforts.

Strategy 2021 - Where do I see myself?

In the first quarter of 2021 we took the chance to bring everyone on the same level of development in a sense that all employees were thinking about their position, their role at Alasco and how they want to grow within the next year. Therefore we designed the Feedback Cycle Q1/2020 to not only look back on 2020, but most importantly preview and anticipate what’s going to come in 2021. This included especially the role each and everyone plays in the strategy presented by the management for the upcoming year. What should be their role? Where lies their ambition and their talents? In what areas should we support them? These are big questions to be answered and we wanted to make sure that after this quarter’s feedback every Alascian feels confident and secure about what is to come.

The Feedback suggestion

Before we dive into the reasons for a company-wide pre-structured feedback, we want to share the email we sent to each People Manager.

This was our feedback suggestion:

Part 1:

The Feedback starts with the usual analysis of the past year and feedback between you and the employee. The following guiding questions will help to structure the first part of the feedback:

  • What have been the successes and accomplishments in the past year/since the employee started at Alasco?
  • How are you feeling about work?
  • What about your job is most satisfying for you?
  • What about work is frustrating?
Part2:

The second part includes the planning and goal-setting for next year. Openly talk with your employee about their areas for potential growth and their development in 2021. Two things that can help with that:

Send your employee the strategy slides for 2021 with the following questions

  • How do you see yourself in this strategy?
  • What tasks do you want to take over with this strategy?
  • What skills or competencies do you want to strengthen and develop in 2021?

Go back to the SKATE-Feedback from the last feedback. Discuss the areas for development with high ambition or talent.

Part 3:

The third part is the Feedback to you as a Team Lead

  • What can I do better or differently as your manager to support you?
  • What did work well in our collaboration? What didn’t?
Part 4:

The first three parts help you to gather data and give you & your employee an idea of the development goals for 2021. Conclude the Feedback by writing down the wishes and ideas both of you came up with. Document the Feedback and use it in the Personal OKR Session and throughout the year to adapt the development plan of the employee.

Part 5:

Use the next 1:1 session to discuss the personal OKRs for Q1/2021. During the next meeting with the P&O Team we will talk with you about your employees and discuss their development.

Evaluating the Feedback

After all Feedback sessions had been conducted, the P&O Team met with all team leads to discuss the feedback they had received and what paths they see for their employees in 2021. We discussed challenges and opportunities and most importantly how we as the P&O Team can support their efforts in developing their team. Further, we also took the time to talk to each employee individually to brainstorm and foster ideas for each and everyone of them. This gave us the full understanding on where everyone stands and what measures we can take in the next 12 month to give everyone the feeling that Alasco is the place where they can see, grow and fulfill their potential.

Why so much hustle?

We have the vision that Alasco is the professional home for our employees where they can develop personally and professionally. The Feedback that we set in Q1 was quite time consuming but it gave us the chance to set the stage for 2021. By talking to both, Team Leads and employees, we now have a deep understanding about their development goals, challenges and ambitions. This makes it far easier for us to establish courses, development paths and to keep track of everyone's growth. We are excited to support and guide our team through their development this year and to see how each and everyone takes more steps towards the position where they want to be in our 2021 strategy.
